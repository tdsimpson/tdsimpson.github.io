About

Background

I've always been drawn to building things people use. I studied computer science to give myself the tools to build, and working across product, engineering, and design, mostly at early-stage startups, has shown me how products come together from every side. What ties all of it together is the user experience itself: how people think, where they get stuck, and what would make a hard task feel easy.

Along the way I've defined features, mapped user flows, tested concepts, written in-app copy, and shipped alongside engineers, across everything from community projects to e-commerce, SaaS, and mobile apps.

If there's one thing I optimize for, it's clarity. In my experience, teams often overestimate how quickly people will understand a product, so it's worth thinking carefully across every touchpoint.
