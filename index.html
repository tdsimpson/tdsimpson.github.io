About

I like working to create products that feel useful, clear, and genuinely helpful - the kinds of tools people can pick up and understand without effort.

Over the past 8 years, my path has taken me through community-driven projects, e-commerce, SaaS products, and mobile apps. I’ve helped define features, map user flows, test concepts, write in-app copy, and collaborate closely with engineers to bring ideas into the real world. Each project has taught me something different about how people think, navigate products, and make decisions.

For me, design isn’t just about how something looks; it’s also about the decisions behind it and how people move through an experience. I enjoy working through problems end-to-end — understanding what people need, shaping how a feature should work, and designing the flows and interfaces that bring it to life.

Outside of work, I love playing my guitar, cooking new recipes, and getting out on the skating rink.
