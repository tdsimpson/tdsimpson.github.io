Problem Definition

During shows people often asked Paper Ladies members Thomas and Joe where they could find them online. They often answered with a long list of different platforms that people couldn’t remember including instagram, youtube, twitter, facebook, spotify, bandcamp.

Product Goal

Paper Ladies wanted to create a central place where fans could go to learn more and stay up to date with their new music and show dates. When people asked where they could find them online, they could answer with “paperladies.ca” instead of naming a list of platforms that did not easily link to one another.

Constaints

  • Embedded Spotify links limit the listening time to 30 seconds.

Conclusion

I designed and developed a simple website for Paper Ladies that included an about section, their latest music, upcoming shows, relevant social media links and a photo gallery. It also included their email and location to help them connect with venues for upcoming shows.
